How could one encourage communication between families?

Dinner table conversations were lackluster in my household. Looking into the American household, I realized how iconic the phrase "Pass the salt" is in our culture. That was my aha moment. The salt and pepper shakers were a perfect symbol for family conversations, and an opportunity for intervention.
